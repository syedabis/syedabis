### 🚀 Expertise

I specialize in building applications powered by **Generative AI** — from LLM-driven chatbots and agents to retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines and prompt/workflow design for production use cases.

Alongside GenAI, I work on **automation**, designing pipelines and tools that remove repetitive manual work and connect AI models into real business workflows end-to-end.

I also work in **computer vision**, building models and pipelines for image/video understanding — from detection and classification to custom vision pipelines tailored to specific use cases.
